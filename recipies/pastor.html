Taco de Pastor

The best firend of Mexicans:'Taco de Pastor'. Les't go

Ingredients

  • Marinated pork meat (usually made with a mix of spices)
  • Small corn tortillas
  • Pineapple slices
  • Chopped onions
  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Lime wedges
  • Salsa (salsa verde or salsa roja, as preferred)

Steps:

  1. Start by marinating the pork meat with a blend of spices, allowing the flavors to infuse for a few hours or overnight.
  2. Cook the marinated pork meat on a grill, rotating and slicing it as it cooks for that signature "al pastor" style.
  3. Warm the small corn tortillas on the grill or in a pan until they are pliable.
  4. Assemble the tacos by placing slices of cooked pork onto each tortilla.
  5. Add a slice of pineapple, a sprinkle of chopped onions, and a few fresh cilantro leaves.
  6. Squeeze a lime wedge over the taco to enhance the flavors.
  7. Top with your preferred salsa, whether it's salsa verde or salsa roja.
  8. Repeat the process for as many tacos as desired.
  9. Serve the "Taco de Pastor" immediately while they are still warm.
  10. Enjoy the delicious and authentic flavors of Mexico!
