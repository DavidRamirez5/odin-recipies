Guacamole

Guacamole is the perfect companion for your tacos. Let's go.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-2 jalapeño peppers, seeds and veins removed, minced
  • 1-2 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1-2 limes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cut the ripe avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a mixing bowl.
  2. Mash the avocado with a fork or potato masher until it reaches your desired level of smoothness.
  3. Add the finely diced onion, minced garlic, and minced jalapeño peppers to the bowl.
  4. Stir in the diced tomatoes and chopped cilantro.
  5. Squeeze the juice of 1-2 limes over the mixture to enhance flavor and prevent browning.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and mix well to combine all the ingredients.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  8. Cover the guacamole with plastic wrap, pressing it directly onto the surface to minimize air exposure.
  9. Refrigerate for about 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  10. Serve the guacamole with your favorite chips, tacos, or as a topping.
  11. Enjoy your homemade guacamole!
